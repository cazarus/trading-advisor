About This Application

Trading Agent v1.0 - Day Trader's Second Brain

A comprehensive web-based trading analysis platform built with principles from "Day Trading 101". The system combines technical analysis, fundamental data, market sentiment, and risk management to score potential trading opportunities.

Features:

  • Real-time market data from multiple sources
  • AI-powered trade opportunity scoring (0-100)
  • Trade journal with P&L tracking
  • Personal knowledge base for trading wisdom
  • Market news and sentiment analysis
  • Risk management compliance checking
  • All data persists in browser localStorage

Disclaimer: This tool is for educational and analysis purposes. Always verify analysis independently and consult a financial advisor before trading. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
